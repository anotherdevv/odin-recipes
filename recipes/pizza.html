Recipe for this Pizza

We're going to be making a regular pizza, but this is going to be a copy cat of pizza huts ingregients. This pizza is going to be a combo with a few meats and veggies. Combo pizza is made by topping a pizza crust with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and a mix of meats (like pepperoni and sausage) and vegetables (such as bell peppers, onions, and olives), then baking until golden and bubbly.
